A job Portal, also known as a career portal, is a website that connects job seekers with potential employers. Job portals allow job seekers to search for jobs, apply for positions, and submit their resumes or CVs. Employers can use job portals to post job openings and receive applications from interested candidates. A job portal is a website or online platform that connects job seekers with potential employers. Job portals can be free or paid, and their main goal is to provide a quick, easy, and online service. Job portals allow job seekers to: Search for job vacancies, Submit their resumes or CVs, and Apply for jobs. Job portals also allow recruiters to post jobs, which can make it easier for companies to find jobs online. Job portals can help automate the recruitment process, and can be an effective way to connect job seekers with recruiters.
Some examples of job portals include: Naukri, Glassdoor, LinkedIn, Monster, Indeed.com, Foundit, Jobs for Her, Shine.com, and Google Jobs.
